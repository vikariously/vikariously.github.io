## Ingredients

- 4 oz. Silver Rum (e.g. Diplomatico Planas or Planteray 3 Stars)
- 2 oz. Lime Juice
- 1 oz. Simple Syrup
- 2 cups frozen sliced plums
- 1 cup ice

## Directions

1. Blend
1. Pour
1. Drink
1. Enjoy
